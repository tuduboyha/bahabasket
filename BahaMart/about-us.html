Our Story

From a Small Idea to a Big Dream

It was 2023. A friend of ours, Ramesh, ran a clothing shop in Gandhi Nagar. He had great products and excellent service — but customers weren't coming in because he had no online presence.

That's when we thought — why not build a platform where every local shopkeeper can showcase their shop online? Without any technical knowledge, without heavy investment. And where customers can place orders directly on WhatsApp — no delivery wait, no return hassles!

Today, BahaBasket has 500+ shops, 10,000+ products, and 25,000+ satisfied customers. And this is just the beginning!

Our Mission, Vision & Values
The three pillars on which BahaBasket stands

Our Mission

To give local shopkeepers in every small town and city of India a powerful digital platform — where they can showcase their products and customers can connect with them directly. Without any technical skill, without heavy expenses.

Our Vision

To bring 50,000+ local shops online across 500 cities in India by 2027, and to build an ecosystem where hyperlocal commerce thrives. Strengthening the local economy is our top priority.

Our Values

Transparency — no hidden charges. Simplicity — anyone can use it. Community — supporting local shops. Trust — creating a safe space for both buyers and sellers.

Our Journey
From the beginning to today — BahaBasket's story at a glance
January 2023

The Idea is Born

After talking to local shopkeepers in Gandhi Nagar, we realised they needed a simple digital presence. That conversation sparked the idea for BahaBasket.

June 2023

Platform Development Begins

A 3-member team started building the platform. The decision to make WhatsApp integration a core feature was made here — and it turned out to be the best decision!

October 2023

First 50 Shops Onboard

50 shops joined at the beta launch. In the very first week, 200+ customer inquiries came through WhatsApp. The sellers were thrilled!

March 2024

100 Shops & 1,000 Products

100 shops and 1,000+ products in just 6 months. Growth was driven entirely by word of mouth — no big marketing budget, just a great product!

August 2024

500 Shops — 5 Cities

500+ shops, expanded to 5 cities. Local media covered our story. We received the "India's Best Hyperlocal Marketplace" award at a digital commerce summit.

Coming Soon 2025

Mobile App & 50 Cities

Android & iOS app launching soon. Expanding to 50 new cities. AI-powered product recommendations and local delivery tracking are also on the way!
