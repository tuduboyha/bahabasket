Frequently Asked Questions

Your answer might already be here!

Is it free to register a shop? +
Yes! On the Basic plan, you can list up to 10 products completely free. No monthly fee, no commission. For more products, paid plans start from ₹499/month.
How does the WhatsApp button work? +
When a customer clicks the "Chat on WhatsApp" button, they are redirected directly to the seller's WhatsApp with a pre-filled message. The seller and customer communicate directly.
Is there an online payment option? +
Currently the platform does not have built-in online payments — this is intentional. Sellers and buyers deal directly on WhatsApp and pay via cash or UPI, which helps maintain local trust.
How long does shop verification take? +
After registration, our team completes verification within 24–48 hours. A confirmation is sent via WhatsApp. For urgent verification, please call us directly.
Can I list my shop in multiple cities? +
Yes! On the Premium plan you can list your shop across multiple locations. The Basic and Growth plans support one city/location each.
How do I upload product photos? +
Go to your Seller Dashboard and use the "Add Product" section to upload photos directly from your phone. Supported formats: JPG/PNG, max 5MB. Multiple photos are allowed.
What is a Featured Listing? +
With a Featured Listing, your shop and products appear at the top of the homepage and search results. This increases visibility and drives more customer inquiries.
How do I submit a complaint? +
Select the "Complaint" topic in the form above and describe your issue in detail. You can also WhatsApp us directly at +91 98765 43210. We resolve complaints within 24 hours.

Need Instant Help? WhatsApp Us!

For any question — product, shop, or technical issue — we are available on WhatsApp 24/7. Average reply time: 15 minutes.
