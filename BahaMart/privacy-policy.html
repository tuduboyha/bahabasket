Seller Guidelines
1Our Seller Standards

BahaBasket maintains a high-quality, trusted marketplace for buyers. All sellers must follow these guidelines to keep their shop listed and in good standing. These guidelines exist to protect both buyers and honest sellers.

Our Promise to Sellers
We are committed to helping local businesses grow digitally. Our team manually verifies every shop, provides WhatsApp inquiry routing, and offers dedicated seller support to help you succeed on the platform.
2Product Listing Standards
  • Use clear, well-lit, original photos of your actual products — stock photos from other websites are not allowed
  • Product title must be accurate and descriptive (e.g., "Men's Cotton Kurta Set – Size S to XXL")
  • Pricing must be the actual selling price inclusive of all taxes
  • Keep product availability updated — mark out-of-stock items promptly
  • Product description must be in English or Hindi — no other languages
  • Do not use watermarks, logos of competitor platforms, or irrelevant images
  • Do not use misleading titles like "MRP ₹5000 Selling at ₹500" unless genuinely discounted
  • Do not list products in the wrong category to gain more visibility
3WhatsApp Communication Standards

Since WhatsApp is the primary communication channel on our platform, sellers must maintain high standards:

  • Respond to all buyer inquiries within 2 hours during shop working hours
  • Be polite, professional, and helpful in all communications
  • Provide accurate information about price, availability, and delivery
  • Set up a WhatsApp Business account with your shop name and logo
  • Do not use buyer contact information for unsolicited marketing after the inquiry is resolved
  • Do not send abusive, threatening, or inappropriate messages to buyers
  • Do not share misleading information about product quality or delivery timelines
4Reviews & Rating Policy

Reviews are critical to buyer trust. The following rules apply to all shop reviews:

  • Only verified buyers who have submitted a genuine WhatsApp inquiry can leave a review
  • All reviews are moderated by our team before being published
  • Sellers may NOT ask friends/family to leave fake positive reviews — this results in immediate suspension
  • Sellers may report fake negative reviews to support@localbazaarhub.in with evidence
  • Sellers may post a public reply to any review through their seller dashboard
