Click "Chat on WhatsApp" — It's That Easy
Found what you want? Click the green "Chat on WhatsApp" button. A pre-filled message opens automatically in WhatsApp with the product name, price, and your inquiry. Just press send — the seller gets your inquiry instantly.
  • Pre-filled message saves you time — no typing needed
  • Message goes directly to the shop owner, not a call center
  • Ask about size, color, availability, and price in one chat
  • Send photos of what you're looking for — sellers love reference photos
  • Most sellers reply within 15–30 minutes during business hours
  • Negotiate price directly — local sellers are always open to a deal
100% Private: We never read your WhatsApp conversations. Everything is end-to-end encrypted between you and the seller.

Once you've agreed on everything via WhatsApp, you have two convenient options — visit the shop for same-day pickup, or get it delivered to your door. Pay directly to the seller using cash or UPI — no platform payment required.

Seller Guide

List Your Shop & Start Getting
WhatsApp Customers

From registration to your first WhatsApp inquiry — get your local shop online in less than 24 hours.

Step 1
Register Your Shop — Free in 5 Minutes
Fill in your shop name, category, address, WhatsApp number, and upload at least one product photo. That's it — your registration is submitted! No documents required for basic listing.
No Documents Needed Takes 5 Minutes Shop Name WhatsApp Number Address 1 Product Photo
Set up a WhatsApp Business account before registering — it gives your shop a professional profile with business hours and quick replies.
Step 2
Our Team Verifies Your Shop (24–48 Hours)
Our Indore team manually reviews every shop registration to ensure quality and authenticity. This protects buyers and honest sellers alike. You'll receive a WhatsApp confirmation once your shop is live.
Business Verification Photo Review WhatsApp Number Check 24–48 Hour Process
Step 3
Add Your Products with Photos & Prices
Once live, log into your Seller Dashboard and start adding products. Upload up to 5 photos per product, set prices, add size/color variants, and write a description. The more products you add, the more inquiries you get!
Up to 5 Photos/Product Size & Color Variants MRP & Selling Price Free: 10 Products Growth: 100 Products Premium: Unlimited
Photo Quality Matters: Shops with 3+ clear product photos get 2.5x more WhatsApp inquiries than shops with 1 photo.
Step 4
Buyers Contact You Directly on WhatsApp
When a buyer clicks "Chat on WhatsApp" on your product, you receive a WhatsApp message with the product details. Respond like a normal WhatsApp chat — discuss price, check availability, share more photos, and arrange delivery.
Direct to Your WhatsApp Pre-filled Buyer Message No Middleman Negotiate Freely Share Photos/Videos
Response Time Matters: Sellers who respond within 30 minutes convert 3x more inquiries into sales than those who reply after 2+ hours.
Step 5
Get Paid Directly — Keep 100% of Revenue
All payments happen directly between you and the buyer via Cash, UPI, or any method you prefer. BahaBasket charges zero commission on sales. Our only fee is the optional monthly subscription for premium features.
0% Commission Cash Payment UPI / GPay Credit/Debit Card Bank Transfer

Grow with Analytics & Premium Features
Track your shop's performance — views, WhatsApp clicks, top products — all from your Seller Dashboard. Upgrade to Growth or Premium plan to rank higher in search, feature on the homepage, and unlock advanced analytics.
Analytics Dashboard Featured Search Listing Homepage Feature Offer Banners Weekly Reports
