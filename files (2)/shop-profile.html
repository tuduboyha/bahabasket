🏪 About Sharma Cloth House

Sharma Cloth House was established in 1998 by Ramesh Sharma in the heart of Gandhi Nagar Market, Indore. With over 26 years of experience in men's fashion, we have become one of the most trusted cloth stores in the city.


We specialize in premium quality cotton kurtas, ethnic wear, formal shirts, and wedding sherwanis. All our products are carefully sourced from reputed manufacturers across Rajasthan, Gujarat, and UP — ensuring the best quality at the most competitive local prices.


Our philosophy is simple — "Customer pehle aata hai." Every customer who walks through our door (or messages us on WhatsApp!) gets personalized attention and honest advice about sizing, fabric, and styling.
