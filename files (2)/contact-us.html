❓ Aksar Pooche Jaane Wale Sawaal

Shayad aapka jawab yahan pehle se ho!

Apni shop register karna free hai kya? +
Haan! Basic plan mein 10 products bilkul free mein list kar sakte hain. Koi monthly fee nahi, koi commission nahi. Zyada products ke liye paid plans ₹499/month se shuru hote hain.
WhatsApp button kaise kaam karta hai? +
Jab customer "Chat on WhatsApp" button click karta hai, toh ek pre-filled message ke saath seedha seller ke WhatsApp pe redirect ho jaata hai. Seller directly customer se baat karta hai.
Kya online payment ka option hai? +
Abhi platform mein online payment nahi hai — ye purposely aisa rakha gaya hai. Seller aur buyer seedha WhatsApp pe deal karte hain aur payment cash ya UPI se karte hain. Isse local trust maintain hota hai.
Shop verify hone mein kitna time lagta hai? +
Registration ke baad 24-48 ghante mein hamari team verification karti hai. WhatsApp pe confirmation aata hai. Urgent verification ke liye seedha call karein.
Kya main multiple cities mein shop list kar sakta hoon? +
Haan! Premium plan mein aap multiple locations pe apni shop list kar sakte hain. Basic aur Growth plan mein ek city/location available hoti hai.
Product photo kaise upload karein? +
Seller dashboard mein jaake "Add Product" section mein directly phone se photo click karke upload kar sakte hain. JPG/PNG format, max 5MB. Multiple photos allowed hain.
Featured listing kya hoti hai? +
Featured listing mein aapki shop aur products homepage pe aur search results mein sabse upar dikhte hain. Isse zyada customers dekhte hain aur inquiries badhti hain.
Koi complaint hai toh kahan karein? +
Complaint ke liye upar form mein "Complaint" topic select karein aur detail mein likhein. Ya seedha WhatsApp karein +91 98765 43210 pe. Hum 24 ghante mein resolve karte hain.
